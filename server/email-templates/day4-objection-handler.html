It's normal to have questions when you're starting your credit repair journey. Here are answers to some of the most common questions we get:

"How long does it take to see results?"

Credit bureaus have 30 days to investigate a dispute. Most users see results within 30-45 days of mailing their first letters.

"Is it legal to dispute items on my credit report?"

Yes! The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) gives you the right to dispute any information on your credit report that you believe is inaccurate, incomplete, or unverified.

"What if a negative item comes back?"

If a removed item reappears, you can re-dispute it. Our software helps you track all your disputes and makes it easy to send follow-up letters.
